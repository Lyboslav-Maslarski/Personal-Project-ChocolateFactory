About Us

The Chocolate Factory was started by a group of friendly folks who wanted to create wonderful chocolate, from bean to bar, everyday. We’re a cozy downtown chocolate factory that scrupulously sources every ingredient to ensure a high quality, responsible chocolate. We are hoping to change the face of fine, hand-crafted, artisan chocolate by being a welcoming chocolate factory that makes people feel happy.

TheChocolateFactory’s doors are open to everyone and anyone curious about the process and taste of handmade chocolate. We believe the factory should be the happiest and most exciting experience possible. People who walk in our doors love the experience of chocolate, and actively seek to understand the source and quality of goods they purchase. Come on down for a visit and don’t forget to taste our delicious chocolate!
